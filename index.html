A Little About the Vyatka Region

Kirov region has long been famous for its rich cultural traditions, which  are manifested in creativity, crafts, hobbies and talents of its residents. The development of these spheres is connected with the history and geographical location of the region, as well as the diverse ethnic composition of its population, and, of course, with the hard work of local residents, who continue   delighting us with their bright works of art.
One of the famous and unique crafts of the Kirov region is the Dymkovo toy. This colorful and bright clay sculpture has become a symbol of the city.
The Kirov region also has such folk crafts as weaving from willow, making wooden toys, embroidery, and others. These crafts are transferred through generations. Many talented musicians, artists, writers, poets, and athletes were born here.
Different festivals and competitions are often held in the region where residents can show their talents.

Elena Shikhova is a craftswoman from Oparino. She delights everyone with her incredible work. She believes that nothing is impossible. All her works are amazing, whether weaving birch, ceramics, painting or patchwork. Each exposition of Elena Shikhova’s exhibitions is a highlight that amazes the audience with both creative intentions and skill.
Elena Shikhova

Elena takes part in many competitions and exhibitions. Elena Shikhova became the winner of the "Master Vyatsky" contest four times!

Elena Rusakova is a famous lace maker from the Kirov region. She wove her first lace at 13! " I love bright lace souvenirs. Shiny white and golden snowflakes, red stars or blue brooches - flowers. Color gives the product brightness and individuality," - says Elena.
Elena Rusakova
"In large products such as collars, shawls or napkins, I use classic color combinations and avoid bright colors, particularly green. I believe that bright colors diminish the nobility and sophistication of these objects."
"At the request of her son Elena herself drew a sketch and weave a small anchor"
It took me 3 days from sketch to finished product. This motif so inspired me," Elena shares in her blog.
